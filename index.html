How Intelligent Product Recommendation Works

Learn how to get the most out of recommending relevant products and services

  1. Visitors browse your site and visit different pages – category pages, product pages, FAQ’s, blog posts and more – depending on their interests and where they are in the buying process.
  2. Meanwhile, our product recommendation engine is analyzing their behavior on your site. After a brief training period, our algorithms precisely identify patterns of behavior when visitors abandon your site.
  3. If a potential customer is about to abandon your site, a perfectly timed message appears automatically, recommending a customized set of products that are most likely to meet their needs.
